About Us

We are London and Essex Vacant, we visit your empty property within London or Essex and ensure your property has not been broken into or vandalised making sure it is fully secure. We will need to access the property to check all the rooms for any water/fire damage, insect infestation, mould and anything that can devalue it. If you have any requests, like mowing the grass, cleaning the house, fixing water/fire damage or removing infestations, these will be outsourced and priced separately. We will be present to overlook the outsource company while the work is carried out at your property.
